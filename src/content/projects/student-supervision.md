I co-supervise Masters and Honours students at the University of Pretoria, typically on studies related to adoption, risk management, and innovation in the built environment and industrial engineering. Topics so far have ranged from open innovation adoption in timber construction to project risk management in South African construction, enterprise risk management at PRASA, and disruptive innovation in start-ups. Several of these projects have produced peer-reviewed publications in *SAJIE* and at the ASOCSA Built Environment Conference.
