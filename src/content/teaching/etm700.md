ETM700 is a postgraduate module at NQF Level 8 covering technology and innovation management, delivered to postgraduate diploma and honours students at the GSTM. I develop the module content: weekly lectures, prescribed and recommended readings, application exercises tied to a recurring case company, and assessments. The work involves translating current research and real industry cases into material that postgraduate students can actually use, which is a different challenge from writing for a journal audience.
