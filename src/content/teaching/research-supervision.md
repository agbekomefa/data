I co-supervise Masters students at the GSTM, typically on topics related to adoption, risk management, and innovation in the built environment or industrial engineering. My role sits alongside the primary supervisor, and I tend to get involved most heavily on methodology (survey design, systematic literature reviews, qualitative research design) and on the writing itself. Several of these students have gone on to co-author peer-reviewed publications, which is one of the more satisfying parts of the work.
