I run training sessions on qualitative research methods and ATLAS.ti for Masters students at the GSTM. What started as a one-off has become a regular departmental request. The sessions cover research design for interview-based studies, thematic analysis, and hands-on use of ATLAS.ti for coding and data management. I draw heavily on my own PhD experience with semi-structured interviews and ATLAS.ti, which makes the training grounded in actual research practice rather than software tutorials.
