I supervise honours students doing their research studies in IGB/ISC 780 at the GSTM. In practice this means guiding students from topic selection through to a completed research report: helping them frame a workable research question, structure a literature review, choose and apply a methodology, and write up their findings. Most of the students I supervise work on topics related to adoption, innovation, or risk management in the built environment and industrial engineering.
