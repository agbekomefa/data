I am starting a project with a friend to help small and medium enterprises in Ghana through the tender and procurement process. The idea grew out of watching SMEs with good capacity lose out on contracts because they did not know how to put a bid together or meet compliance requirements. We are still in the early stages, and more details will follow as the project takes shape.
