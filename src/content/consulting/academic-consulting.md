I do consulting work for postgraduate students who need help with their theses and dissertations. The work is substantive: interpreting supervisor or examiner feedback, restructuring arguments, reworking theoretical framing, or helping a student get a chapter from a rough draft to something defensible. I also help with methodology, particularly survey design, systematic literature reviews, and qualitative research.

The consulting comes out of my own research experience (published papers, a PhD nearing completion, co-supervision of several students through to publication) and a genuine interest in helping people produce work they can be proud of.
